Lleida

In 1979 the White Stork was facing extinction in Catalonia, only 6 pairs left. The bird is legally protected in Catalonia now and significant effort has been developed to conserve storks, especially in urban areas. Due to this effort, Catalonia experienced strong growth of the White Stork population and thousands of storks settles this area nowadays. While Lleida has a stable population of 360 pairs many others visit the city during the migration period.
